What is wireless charging?

Wireless charging uses inductive energy transfer technology - QI standard. This standard was developed by a consortium of wireless electromagnetic energy to transmit energy at a distance of up to 4 cm without wires.

Some manufacturers, such as Sony, Smasung, LG already use this technology in their smartphones and if the manufacturer of your smartphone has deprived it of such a function, you can add this function by purchasing a QI kit in our online store. The kit includes a charger and receiver for the phone, which allows you to add the function of wireless charging, almost any device!
